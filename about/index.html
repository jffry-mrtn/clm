"Serve another with the gifts each of you received, thus becoming good managers of the varied graces of God" -1 Peter 4:10

Event Planning Professional Caroline Martin will be your partner to ensure that your occasion will be memorable and treasured for a lifetime.

After years of experience of coordinating government functions, Caroline professionally enters the exciting event planning industry as a certified International Event and Wedding Planner. Caroline wants to share the passion, expertise and knowledge she has gained through nearly three decades in Foreign Service work.

Caroline's Message to You

I was born to organize and my close friends call me a social butterfly. Thus, I have decided to bring myself, my core skills and values to a higher level, fulfill my dream to establish a business and at the same time serve my community!

In today's fast-paced world, planning a party, or, just about any event is a time consuming process. Hosting parties has become more complicated than ever --- food, activities, and decorations to think of. Every detail matters.

This is where I come in. My main desires are for every party host or hostess to enjoy his or her own party and have a great time reconnecting with their guests, and for the celebration to be a cherished memory for everyone!

Allow me to explore many different ways you and I can work together. I promise you that after our collaboration, you will choose me as your partner in your events. Whether it be conceptualizing, product selection, or perhaps simply overseeing that all runs smoothly on the day, your event will turn out extra special with me by your side.

As no event is the same, CLM Events Services is able to adapt to its clients' particular needs and requirements.
